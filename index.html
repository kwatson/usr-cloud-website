usr.cloud

Free hosted services enabling you to take back control of your data.
We won't sell your data, and there are no advertisements. This project is funded through donations, and managed by volunteers.
We provide Matrix for secure communications, Jitsi for online meetings, and Mastodon for social media.

Proudly hosted in Oregon.

Where is my data located?
All data is stored in Portland, OR. Backups are encrypted, and sent offsite to Virginia.
How is this funded?
This is a volunteer run service, and the infrastructure is paid for by generous donations from our community members.
What services do you offer?
  • Chat (Matrix & Element)
  • Mastodon (learn more)
  • Online Meetings

Mastodon

You may either register on the website, or in the mobile app.
What is Mastodon?
Mastodon is an open-source social media software platform (more like twitter than facebook).
How do I get started?
You may create your account by signing up on our website, or by downloading a mastodon supported mobile app and signing up there.

Matrix (Chat)

What is Matrix?
Matrix is a distributed, federated, and encrypted chat services (similar to slack). You can chat with your friends on your computer or phone, or join group chats and communities.
How do I get started?
You may create your account by signing up on our website, or by downloading a mobile or desktop app.
